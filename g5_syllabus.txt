

 
How to Use the Pacing and Pathways Guide 
The Go Math! Pacing and Pathways Guide offers teachers the flexibility to tailor their instructional pacing by selecting from 
three distinct pathways. Among these, Pathway 3 is the recommended option, providing 160 days of comprehensive math 
instruction. Pathways 1 and 2 are also available, allowing teachers to focus on the most relevant course content based on 
classroom performance throughout the year. 
 
Pathway Options 
 
Pathway 3 (Recommended): This pathway covers all grade-level standards and includes both essential and supporting 
areas of emphasis. Lessons in Pathway 3 include all grade-level content and are indicated by the п‚ў, в–Ў and Рћ symbols.
 
Pathway 2: This pathway includes all essential lessons that teach priority content covering the main areas of emphasis for 
each grade, indicated by п‚ў symbol.  It also includes lessons that address supporting clusters, indicated by the в–Ў symbol. 
 
Pathway 1: This pathway focuses on essential lessons that teach priority content covering the main areas of emphasis for each 
grade. These lessons are indicated by the п‚ў symbol and should take up the majority of instructional time.
